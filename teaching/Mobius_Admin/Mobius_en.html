Möbius for Instructors

Introduction

You should consult all the topics under the section of the menu above if you plan to seriously use Möbius for the assignments in your course.

Since all of you will use BrightSpace for your courses, you must also consult all the topics under the section of the menu above. You will learn how to setup your Möbius assignments in BrightSpace to enssure that the grades are properly transferred to BrightSpace.

Once you course in Möbius has been created, you may want to go under Class Info ==> Edit to modify the section Class Message. You MUST NOT MODIFY any other item. If the following text doesn't appear at the top of the website for your course, we strongly suggest to add it.

  • English: You must access your assignments using only the link within Brightspace or else your grades will not be transferred to Brightspace.
  • Français: Vous devez accéder aux devoirs à l'aide du lien dans BrightSpace seulement faut de quoi vos notes ne seront pas transférées à BrightSpace.
