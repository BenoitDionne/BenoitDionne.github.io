BrightSpace Module for Möbius Assignments

We will not explain how to create a module on BrightSpace in this page. We assume that the instructor knows how to create a module on Möbius. We focus on the minimal content of this module.

The introduction of your module for the assignments must include a link to the . On the FAQ website, students will find the instructions to buy the licence for Möbius. In particular, they will find the browser requirements. The non-compatibility of the browser is the most frequent source of problems. Students must ensure that their browser accepts third party cookies. You must also ensure that at least your first assignments open in a new tab, not inside BrightSpace. In fact, it is preferable that they all open in a new tab. It is then easier for students to read long questions.

Obviously, it is in this module that you will include the links to your assignments on Möbius. We explain how to do so on the site .

In addition to the LTI links from BrightSpace to the assignments on Möbius, you must also create the LTI link from BrightSpace to the Gradebook on Möbius. Consult the site for the instruction to do it. It is through Gradebook on Möbius that students will access the detailed solutions of their assignments.
