How to Use Möbius with BrightSpace

Getting started

You must use Chrome as browser (Firefox is no longer supported by Möbius). You must also make sure that third party cookies are accepted by your browser. For more information on allowing third party cookies on your browser, please consult the site .
Note: Apple computers are notoriously problematic because they do not respect standards. The browser Safari is to be avoided.
  • You must first create your course on Möbius and a LTI link in BrightSpace to the homepage on Möbius for your course. To do that, follow the instructions on the page . You must always access Möbius with this link to the homepage on Möbius. This is where you will go to create your assignments/exams. If you do not have a user account on Möbius, at the same time that you create this link, your instructor account on Möbius will be created using your user account on BrightSpace.
  • Second, you must create a module on BrightSpace for the LTI links to your Möbius assignments. We assume that you know how to create modules on BrightSpace. If you do not, consult the section Adding Modules to Your Course on the site . We provide some information on what should go in that module on the site .
  • Third and last, you must add a link to the Gradebook on Möbius in your module for the Möbius assignments. The instructions on how to do that can be found on the site . Students will use this link to access the detailed solutions of their assignments after the deadlines.

Adding Assignments

  • I assume that you don't want to write your own questions but that you want to use the bank of questions that has already been created. The page explains how to preview these questions. You can select some of them for your assignment. Note that you MUST NOT make all the questions public. Those questions are not for practice but for testing. Use the questions in your class textbook for practice.
  • We provide the instructions to create assignments on Möbius on the pages and .
  • You will find on the page the instructions to create a link in BrightSpace to your assignment in Möbius.
